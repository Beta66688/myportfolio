Sound Roaming Plan

An artistic interactive installation

Description

This is a world of sound. Leave your voice here, along with a sentence to accompany it. We will generate a unique QR code for you. Save the QR code, send it to the backend, and we will receive your voice.

Then we will 'print' your voice and place it at the exhibition site; When a destined person appears to take it away, it will mark the beginning of your voice's journey. Narrate, listen, medium, blend.

Through online promotion and offline installations, audiences can obtain a unique electronic music CD by clicking on the webpage, uploading audio, and generating a personalized QR code.

Event Flow

The event flow demonstrates how users interact with the Sound Roaming Plan installation, from initial engagement to the completion of the sound exchange process.

Online Promotional Planning

The online promotional strategy includes story planning and social media engagement to attract participants and build a community around the sound exchange concept.
