Catastrophe Merely a fresh balance

About This Project

This is a visual novel game demo that explores themes of balance and catastrophe. A human is exiled to a deserted area. His portable sensor shows that a beast tide will sweep across his location in 10 days. To evade the beast tide and locate a refuge, he must figure out a way to survive in this deserted area. However, you are not a human; you are one of the animals lurking around human, seeking a way to survive...

Game Type

Visual Novel, Choice-Based Narrative, Indie Game
